Our Story

Founded in 2022, Zipper Sells started as a small digital gaming store built by a group of passionate gamers and developers who wanted to make digital content accessible to everyone worldwide. From day one, our goal was simple — provide instant, secure, and affordable access to games and subscriptions without the typical hassle of waiting hours or risking fake sellers.

Today, we’re trusted by thousands of gamers and content creators. Our store offers top titles like Fortnite, Valorant, CS:GO, GTA V, FIFA 26, and subscriptions for Netflix, Discord Nitro, and NordVPN — all delivered instantly with automated systems.

Our Mission

At Zipper Sells, we believe gaming is more than a hobby — it’s a community. Our mission is to empower gamers by providing trusted products, instant delivery, and 24/7 support. Every click and purchase is secured through advanced encryption and global payment partners.

We’re constantly evolving, partnering with game publishers and tech brands to deliver you the latest digital experiences. We don’t just sell keys — we sell access to your next adventure.
